Delete My Data

If you would like KundaliScribe to delete your personal data, you may submit a deletion request using the details below.

Email for deletion requests: kundaliscribe@gmail.com

Please send your request from the same email address used for your order whenever possible.

What to include in your request

  • Your full name
  • Email address used for the order
  • Report reference ID or order details, if available
  • Your request to delete your personal data

What happens next

We will review your request and may ask for limited information to verify that you are the rightful requester. Once verified, we will process deletion within a reasonable period, subject to legal, fraud-prevention, payment-dispute, and security retention requirements.

What may still be retained

Even after a deletion request is processed, KundaliScribe may retain limited records where required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, payment dispute handling, abuse prevention, messaging diagnostics, or security purposes.
