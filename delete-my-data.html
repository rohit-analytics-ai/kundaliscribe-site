If you would like KundaliScribe to delete your personal data, you may submit a deletion request using the contact details below.

This page is provided to help you exercise your privacy rights under applicable data protection law, and to support app-store disclosure requirements including Google Play's data deletion policy.

How to Request Deletion

Email your deletion request to kundaliscribe@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

For faster processing, please send the request from the same email address that you used for your KundaliScribe order whenever possible.

What to Include

To help us locate your records and process your request efficiently, please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address used for the order
  • Your report ID or order reference, if available (format: KUN-XXXXXXXX)
  • A short note requesting deletion of your personal data
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Hello,

I am requesting deletion of my personal data associated with KundaliScribe.

Full Name:
Order Email Address:
Report ID / Order Reference (if available):
Additional Details:

Please delete my personal data associated with this request, subject to any legal, security, fraud-prevention, or payment-dispute retention requirements.

What Happens Next

1
Acknowledgement — within 48 hours
We will confirm receipt of your request and may ask for limited information to verify that you are the rightful requester.
2
Processing — within 7 business days
Once verified, we will process deletion of your personal data from our systems and, where applicable, request deletion from our data processors.
3
Confirmation
We will send you a confirmation once deletion is complete.

What May Still Be Retained

Even after a deletion request is processed, KundaliScribe may retain limited records where required or permitted by applicable law, including for:

  • Legal compliance and regulatory obligations
  • Fraud prevention and abuse detection
  • Payment dispute handling and transaction records
  • Security incident investigation
  • Message delivery diagnostics

Where retention is required, we will retain only the minimum data necessary and will not use it for any other purpose.
