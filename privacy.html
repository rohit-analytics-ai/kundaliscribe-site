KundaliScribe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use the KundaliScribe Android and iOS application, website, and related services.

By using KundaliScribe, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

KundaliScribe uses server-side astrology calculation and AI-generated narrative services to create both preview outputs and premium reports. Birth details you submit for report generation are processed through our systems and trusted service providers as described below. Report content is generated using large language model AI systems and is not reviewed by a human astrologer unless explicitly stated.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and deliver your personalised Vedic birth chart report:

  • Full Name — used to personalise your chart report
  • Date of Birth — required for planetary position calculations
  • Time of Birth — required for ascendant and house calculations
  • Place of Birth — used to determine geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) and timezone needed for chart calculations
  • Email Address — used to deliver your PDF report and related service communications
  • Hashed device identifier — a non-reversible cryptographic hash of your device ID, used for rate-limiting, abuse prevention, and anonymous conversion analytics. This identifier cannot be used to directly identify you but may be considered personal data under some jurisdictions.
  • Technical and operational data — such as app version, hashed IP address, push notification token, browser or app diagnostic logs, consent timestamps, and order reference identifiers, used for abuse prevention, purchase validation, reliability, troubleshooting, message delivery, and audit logging.
We do not collect your phone number, contacts, photos, biometric data, or social media profiles unless you voluntarily provide such information in a support inquiry. We also do not access your device GPS for chart calculations; location-related inputs are based on the birth details you provide.

KundaliScribe does not require user account registration to access report generation services.

KundaliScribe does not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes and does not link collected personal information with third-party advertising networks.

We do not use your submitted birth details for unrelated profiling or targeted advertising.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely as needed to:

  • Calculate your Vedic birth chart, including planetary positions, houses, yogas, doshas, and related astrological details
  • Generate preview content and personalised AI-written reports using large language model AI systems
  • Deliver your PDF report to the email address you provide
  • Process your in-app purchase through Google Play Billing or Apple App Store billing services, depending on your platform
  • Respond to follow-up support requests you send regarding your purchased report
  • Maintain service security, performance, reliability, fraud prevention controls, transaction validation, and consent audit records
  • Send service-related push notifications, where enabled on your device, such as order status, report readiness, delivery updates, reminders, or important service notices
  • Enforce our age eligibility requirements and maintain records of consent provided through the application

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the service you requested.

No full birth data or personal identifiers beyond those listed are stored in our consent records.

These records are maintained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and to demonstrate that appropriate disclosure was made and accepted prior to report generation.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us without appropriate guardian consent, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it promptly.
